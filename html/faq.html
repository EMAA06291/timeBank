Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Who can use the Time Bank platform?

Anyone who wants to exchange services using time as currency can use the platform. Whether you're a student, a professional, or a retiree, you can offer your skills and request help from others.

How does the time credit system work?

For every hour you spend helping someone, you earn one time credit. You can use that credit later to request an hour of service from another member.

Can I chat with someone before accepting an exchange?

Yes, users can send messages and chat before confirming an exchange. This helps both parties agree on the service details and schedule.

Is this only for local exchanges?

No, the Time Bank supports both local and remote exchanges. If the service can be provided online (like tutoring or design), you can collaborate with people from anywhere.
